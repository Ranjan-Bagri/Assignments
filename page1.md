## To find the sum and average of a list of numbers


### Description:

If a set of \\(n\\) numbers is represented by \\((a_1,a_2,\cdots,a_n)\\), then the sum of \\(n\\) numbers will be
\\[ s & = a_1+a_2+\cdots+a_n \\]
\\[   & = \sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i \\]
where \\(n\in\rm I\!R\\)
